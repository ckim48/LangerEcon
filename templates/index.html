Instruction for Project Langer Economic Tasks

Step 1

You will have $5000 as a budget to buy items for your home. There 4 categories for each of 4 stores. Your task is to buy every 16 items with the limited budgets.

Step 3

After you bought every item, click "Finish Task". Then we will analyze your shopping patterns for you.
